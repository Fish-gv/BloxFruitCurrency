Blox Fruits: Money


Purpose: Currency

Release Date: January 16, 2019 (Update 1)

Max Amount of Money: 1,000,000,000

Can be used in all of the 3 Seas.

It is impossible to give an exact value for how much Money is
needed to fully unlock and max everything out.


Obtainment

- Defeating Enemies
- Defeating Bosses
- Completing Quests
- Hunting a Pirate as a Marine after taking a wanted poster.
- Collecting Chests
- Defeating a YouTuber that gives you a title
- Completing Sea Events
- Purchasing with Robux
- Being gifted money from another player
- Trading for one time money
- Redeeming certain codes
- Opening Fruit Boxes
- Rolling a random surprise from Death King


Usage

- Purchase Items
- Purchase Boats
- Purchase Blox Fruits
- Purchase Abilities
- Purchase Raid Chips
- Unlock fighting styles


Gamepasses

Getting the 2x Money Gamepass will double the money
earned from all ways of obtainment.


Titles

3 titles can be obtained related to Money:

"The Rich" - Obtaining $5M+
"Unlimited Money"- Obtaining $20M+
"The Richest in the World" - Obtaining $50M+
